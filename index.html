About Me

Hey, my name is Justin Cook. I am in my final semester at the University of Florida in the Master of Mass Communication program with a concentration in Digital Strategy. I have worked at Allstate for over six years and enjoy helping others develop personally and professionally. I most recently created a professional development program that we are set to pilot in 2024, where we go out in the community and provide educational opportunities to nonprofits to help their employees and the communities they serve develop themselves further as professionals.
